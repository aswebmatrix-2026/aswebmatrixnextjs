ABOUT Taarzan

OUR STORY

Taarzan Motors was founded in Milan by racing engineer Alessandro Taarzan with a singular vision: to create automobiles that deliver an emotional connection between driver and machine. What started as a small workshop has grown into a global luxury brand, yet every Taarzan vehicle is still hand-assembled with the same passion and attention to detail.

Today, we combine Italian design flair with German engineering precision, creating cars that are both beautiful and brutally fast. Our dedicated team of 2,500 engineers, designers, and craftsmen work tirelessly to push the boundaries of what's possible.
